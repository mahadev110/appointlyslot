About Sarah

Sarah is a passionate interior designer with over 8 years of experience transforming spaces into beautiful, functional environments. She holds a Master's degree in Interior Design from NIFT Mumbai and has worked with over 200 satisfied clients across residential and commercial projects.
Her design philosophy centers on creating spaces that reflect the client's personality while maintaining functionality and aesthetic appeal. Sarah specializes in modern minimalist designs, sustainable interior solutions, and space optimization for small apartments.
